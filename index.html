About Me

Where I am from.
I was born and raised in Ramsey, Minnesota. My entire family is from the cold midwest. I am used to all the seasons at their extremes. I now am in the Inland Empire of California. I did most of my upper level schooling in the Los Angeles Area. I love it here in California, but I do not want to live here forever. I want to explore new places and see what is out there.
How would I describe myself.
I would describe myself as someone hard working and always ready to learn. The more I learn the more excited I become. In my personal life I am caring and try to brighten up the day with a little bit of humor. I am very logic oriented, I love math, science and programming. I also love to think outside of the box. I have always been a team player, and I naturally find myself in leadership positions.

Who do I want to become.
I want to be a provider. I want to make enough money to raise an incredible family, and I want to have extra to explore all of the wonders of Life I want to be someone that people look up to. I want to be able to work enough where my family doesn't have to. I want to be a leader. I want to be a role model.
