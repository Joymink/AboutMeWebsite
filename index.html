About Me

Where I am from.
I was born and raised in Ramsey, Minnesota. My entire family is from the cold midwest. I am used to all the seasons at their extremes. I now am in the Inland Empire of California. I did most of my upper level schooling in the Los Angeles Area. I love it here in California, but I do not want to live here forever.
How would I describe myself.
I would describe myself as someone hard working and always ready to learn. In my personal life I am caring and try to brighten up the day witha little bit of humor. I am very logic oriented, I love math and science and programming. I also love to think outside of the box. I have always been a team player, and I naturally find myself in leadership positions.
